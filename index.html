Arlo - The Good Dinosaur

In an alternate reality where dinosaurs are civilized. Arlo, a kind hearted dinosaur set out on a journey to find his lost home. On the road, he made a human friend, experienced the wild life and grew through the footsteps of his loving father. On the way, he rescued a wild human child and made friend with some T-Rex. He knew that he will one day be able to find his home. He followed the mountain crest which can also be seen from his home. At last, he found his home and family. His little human friend, which he named spot, also reunited with his family. They both lived happily ever after.

Character Description

Arlo

An Apatosaurus

Pixar takes you on an epic journey into the world of dinosaurs where Arlo makes an unlikely human friend. While traveling through a harsh and mysterious landscape, Arlo learns the power of confronting his fears and discovers what he is truly capable of.

Spot

A Primitive Human Child

Spot is a wild, tough, and tenacious human boy who has lived alone in the wilderness for much of his life. He speaks in only grunts and growls, but his strengths are clear: he’s fearless, confident and a survivalist. Spot quickly proves to be a great resource to Arlo, and slowly, becomes a friend. It is unclear what race Spot belongs to, but it is speculated that he is of Mediterranean ethnicity.
